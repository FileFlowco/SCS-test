Our Story

Built on detail,
driven by results.

Soft Cleaning Services started with a simple observation: too many cleaning services deliver "fine" results. Fine isn't what clients deserve. Clients deserve spotless.

We built our business around a detail-first philosophy — using structured checklists, quality products, and a genuine commitment to the kind of clean that actually makes a difference. The kind where you walk in and just feel it.

Based in Jacksonville, FL, we serve residential and commercial clients throughout the area. Whether it's a weekly maintenance visit or a full deep clean, we approach every job with the same standard of excellence.

When you book with Soft Cleaning, you're not just getting a cleaner — you're getting a team that takes pride in making your space shine.
