FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
+
Not at all! Many of our clients provide us with a key or entry code. We're fully insured and trustworthy. You can go about your day and come back to a spotless home.
What products do you use?
+
We use professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning products that are tough on dirt but safe for your family, pets, and surfaces. If you have specific product preferences or sensitivities, just let us know.
How do I get a quote?
+
Simply fill out our contact form or give us a call. Tell us about your space (size, type, what you need) and we'll get back to you same-day with a fair, transparent quote — no obligations.
What areas do you serve?
+
We serve Jacksonville and all surrounding areas including Orange Park, Fleming Island, Ponte Vedra, Jacksonville Beach, St. Augustine, and more. Contact us if you're unsure about your location.
What if I'm not satisfied with the results?
+
Your satisfaction is our guarantee. If anything doesn't meet your expectations, contact us within 24 hours and we'll come back to re-clean the areas in question at no extra charge. We stand behind our work.
How often should I schedule cleanings?
+
Most residential clients opt for bi-weekly service, which keeps your home consistently clean with manageable cost. Weekly service is great for busy families. Monthly deep cleans work well for some households. We'll recommend what's best for your situation.

Send us your address and preferred day — we'll get back to you same-day with availability and a quote. No commitments, no pressure.
